Overview

This summer school will feature three weeklong short courses led by experts, covering topics such as free boundary problems, geometric PDE, stochastic PDE and variational methods to fluids equations. Participants will have the opportunity to present their work through short talks and poster sessions. The primary goal is to equip graduate students with a strong foundation in key research areas of elliptic PDE, foster a sense of community, and encourage future collaborations.
